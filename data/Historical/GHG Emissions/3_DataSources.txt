(3) Green House Gas Emissions

GA_AnnualEmissions.csv
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Type: Time Series
Spatial Region: Georgia
Description: Mass of CO2, SO2, NOx, and Steam Loads in short tons from 2016 to 2022
Data Source: https://campd.epa.gov/
Methodology: Generator level data is summed across all GA emitting plants
Note: Generation data has been cross validated with EIA data. Missing Data for one point
